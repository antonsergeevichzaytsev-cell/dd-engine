# Compliance Overlay

**Purpose.** Per-jurisdiction language blocks for the Author Credentials and Methodology sections of every DD. Drafted to support Russia-resident advisor relationship with Western/international buyers under Strategy v3 "не врать" positioning.

**Important.** This is operational language for use in DDs. It is NOT legal advice. Material engagement with sanctioned jurisdictions or sanctioned counterparties requires independent counsel — engage Russian and target-jurisdiction lawyers before contracting.

**Update cadence.** Review every 6 months and after any major sanctions package or jurisdictional rule change.

Insert in §2.2 Methodology and §2.3 Compliance of any DD.

## Standing language to ALWAYS avoid

These framings appear professional but create compliance and integrity risk. Do not use under any circumstance:

- "Inside" or "insider information" — implies MNPI access. Frame as "public-source expert interpretation."
- "Network of contacts at [company]" — implies current employee information sharing. Frame as "industry relationships built over 16 years."
- "What Bloomberg doesn't tell you" or similar — implies superior non-public data. Frame as "expert interpretation of public data."
- "Confidential" stamps on DD outputs unless the report contains client-confidential information. Over-use undermines the value of the stamp when it matters.
- "No NDA" caveat in screening calls — implies that with NDA, more sensitive material is available. The "more sensitive material" should not exist outside what is legitimately citable.

## Specific representations that MUST be true at time of signing each DD

Verify and document each, with date stamp, before issuing any DD:

1. **Author SDN status check.** OFAC SDN List, OFAC Consolidated, EU consolidated, UK OFSI, Swiss SECO, Australian DFAT, Japanese METI, Canadian SEMA. Date-stamped screenshot or written log.
2. **Author UBO chain.** Confirm sole-trader status (ИП) without external beneficial ownership. Document.
3. **No compensation from target.** Document agreement structure showing buyer-only compensation flow.
4. **No conflict of interest.** Author has not advised the target or its affiliates within prior 24 months. Document with disclosure to buyer.
5. **No use of confidential prior-employer information.** Author certifies in writing for each DD.

## Sanctioned-asset DD — special handling

If the target asset, its operator, its parent, or any UBO is on any sanctions list:

- **Do not proceed without independent sanctions counsel engagement, both Russian and buyer-jurisdiction.**
- Compensation structure requires General Licence verification (US) or specific licence (EU/UK/Swiss).
- DD output should be circulated only to buyer's pre-cleared compliance team.
- Engagement should be papered with explicit sanctions representations from buyer.
- Strong consideration to declining the engagement. Long-term risk exceeds single-engagement revenue.

## Personal-data and confidentiality side

In addition to sanctions overlay:

- DD must not include personal data of identifiable individuals beyond what is necessary for the asset assessment.
- Officers' names, biographical detail beyond role descriptions, internal stakeholder identities — must not be included in widely circulated DD versions. Reserve for private retainer outputs under explicit scope.
- Regulation GDPR (EU buyers) and analogous regimes apply to personal data flow even when sanctions framework permits the engagement.
